Project Status: Beta Version (Work in Progress)
Project Idea This project is a 2D Motion Graphics promotional video designed for the "Waffarha" application. The video addresses the common daily struggle of traditional shopping, depicting the physical exhaustion, heavy lifting, and time consumption users face in supermarkets.
The core concept is to present the "Waffarha" app as the ultimate solution that transforms this stressful experience into a convenient one . through a narrative-driven approach, the video highlights key application features such as home delivery , safety , order tracking via map , and continuous discounts. The project concludes with a clear call to action to download the app from the App Store or Google Play , reinforcing the brand slogan: "Save your time... Save your money... Waffarha!".
